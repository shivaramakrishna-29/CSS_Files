  10. Apples are one of the most popular fruits in the world.

Banana

Benefits of Banana :

  1. Banana is a soft and sweet fruit.
  2. It has a yellow peel and creamy texture inside.
  3. Bananas are rich in potassium and vitamins.
  4. They are good for energy and muscle health.
  5. Eating bananas helps in improving digestion.
  6. Bananas grow in tropical climates.
  7. They are used to make shakes, smoothies, and desserts.
  8. Bananas have a naturally sweet and pleasant taste.
  9. They are low in fat and easy to digest.
  10. Bananas are one of the most widely consumed fruits in the world.
